Ciao! I’m a self-taught photographer originally from Bangladesh, raised in Italy, and currently residing in Bath, UK. My passion for photography has been a constant since my early teens, driving me to continuously explore and refine my skills.

Over the years, I have immersed myself in the art of capturing moments, learning about composition, mastering new techniques, and perfecting the art of retouching. My portfolio spans a diverse range of subjects, from stunning landscapes and dynamic events to intimate portraits.

Thank you for taking the time to view my work. I hope you enjoy exploring my portfolio and the website I developed as much as I enjoyed creating them. If you’d like to learn more about my journey, discuss potential collaborations, or enquire about my photography services for events such as weddings and parties, please feel free to get in touch. I am fluent in Bengali, Italian, and English, and I look forward to connecting with you.
